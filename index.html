    my story

    My name is Jessica. I am a Dutch creative person who loves to tell stories. As a child, I read every book I could get my hands on. Later, I discovered YouTube and this is where my love of online video began. Eager to learn more about storytelling and video, I decided to study Film at the University of Amsterdam. This gave me a great theoretical foundation in film and allowed me to study at the University of Kent, where I could expand my knowledge.

    Back in my home country, my hands were itching to create content myself. This is when I picked up video editing and started doing as many projects as possible to gain experience. Besides video editing I also picked up design, animation and game art. I always try to keep learning and expanding my skill set, because learning new things is what I love!

    With Content for Good, I hope to create visuals that contribute to building a better world. Originating from my love of good stories, I want to spend my time telling stories that are impactful and important to be told. My focus is not on getting the viral. It’s on telling a good story.
